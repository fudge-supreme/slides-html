Meet Sofia

Sofia
Facialist

Glow Spa
Beauty Center

Wow! This is Sofia. She is a young and talented facialist. Sofia works at Glow Spa — a small but beautiful beauty center in the city.

This spa is near the main plaza. It is close to the park, but far from the highway. That big building across the street is a hotel, but this building is Sofia's favorite place in the world.

Sofia loves her job. She always shows up early. She doesn't like to be late. These are her tools: clean towels, small brushes, bottles, and special cream. Soft music always plays inside the spa. Oh, it is a magical place!

Present Simple (+): subject + verb(+s) → She works / Music plays  |  Present Simple (−): subject + doesn't + base verb → She doesn't like to be late.
Slide 02 / 13📖 Story — Part 2

Glow Spa

Glow Spa is more relaxing than a regular clinic. It is cleaner than most places in the city. In fact, it is the most beautiful spa in the neighborhood.

These bottles on the shelf are new — they contain organic cream and natural oil. Those old cabinets in the corner have extra supplies. Sofia doesn't use chemical products — she believes in natural beauty. Well, that is a great philosophy!

Sofia sets up the treatment room every morning. She turns on the soft music and the warm lamp. The room is small, but it is more comfortable than a big hospital room. It is the most peaceful place Sofia knows.

Comparatives: adj + -er than / more + adj + than → cleaner than / more relaxing than
Superlatives: the + adj + -est / the most + adj → the most beautiful / the most peaceful
Slide 03 / 13📖 Story — Part 3

Sofia's Morning Routine

Sofia wakes up early every day. She puts on her white uniform and picks up her bag. She shows up at the spa at 8:00 AM, because she likes to prepare everything before the clients come in.

She usually makes a cup of tea, but she doesn't eat at work. She quickly cleans the treatment table and checks her schedule. She has five clients today — that is a busy day!

Sofia works hard, but she loves every moment. She is a professional, so she never gives up. She takes care of every detail, because she wants the best results for her clients.

Adverbs of frequency: always → usually → sometimes → never
She always shows up early.  /  She usually makes tea.  /  She never gives up.

A Morning at Work

📋

Right now, it is 10:00 AM. Sofia is applying a thick cream to Maria's face. Maria is lying comfortably on the treatment table. The cream is working on Maria's skin slowly and gently.

Sofia is not talking loudly. She is mixing two products in a small bowl. She is looking after Maria very attentively. Next to the table, there is a glass of water and some cotton pads.

The receptionist, Lena, is coming in to the room. She is carrying a new bottle of serum. "Sofia, we are running out of the rose oil!" she says. Sofia is working on a solution — she is not giving up.

Present Continuous (+): is/are + verb-ING → She is applying cream.
Present Continuous (−): isn't/aren't + verb-ING → She is not talking loudly.
Present Continuous (?): Is/Are + subject + verb-ING? → Is she mixing products?
Slide 05 / 13📖 Story — Part 5

A Conversation

(Sofia finishes the treatment. Maria opens her eyes and smiles.)

Maria:"Wow! Sofia, my skin is feeling so soft! Is this the new cream?"
Sofia:"Yes, it is. This is our best organic cream. Those are the results after only one session!"
Maria:"Oh! Really? Does it work for sensitive skin?"
Sofia:"Of course! It doesn't contain chemical additives. It is very gentle. That is why your skin looks so smooth right now."
Maria:"Well… Is the next appointment far from today?"
Sofia:"Not at all! We have availability next week. This treatment is the most effective one we offer, and you are going to love the results!"
Maria:"Great! I always come back to Glow Spa. It is the best place in the city!"
Questions — Present Simple: Does + subject + base verb? → Does it work?
Questions — Present Continuous: Is/Are + subject + verb-ING? → Is she applying it?
Slide 06 / 13📖 Story — Part 6

The End of the Day

It is 6:00 PM. Sofia is putting away the products and is cleaning up the treatment room. She doesn't leave anything on the table. She picks up the towels and places them in the basket.

Today is a great day. Sofia has five happy clients. She is the most careful facialist at Glow Spa. Her treatments are more effective than those at other spas, and her clients always come back.

"This job is the best job in the world," Sofia thinks. She looks at her reflection in the mirror. Her skin is as soft as her clients'. She takes care of herself too — because a good facialist never gives up on her own skin. Amazing! 🌟
