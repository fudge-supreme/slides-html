01 — Gramática base

Parts of Speech

El inglés organiza las palabras en 8 categorías gramaticales. Conocerlas es la clave para entender cómo funciona cualquier oración.

Noun
Names a person, place, thing or idea.
teacher, city, love
Verb
Expresses action or state of being.
run, is, feel
Adjective
Describes or modifies a noun.
happy, cold, tall
Adverb
Modifies a verb, adjective or adverb.
quickly, very, now
Pronoun
Replaces a noun to avoid repetition.
I, you, he, she
Preposition
Shows relationship between words.
in, on, at, from
Conjunction
Connects words, phrases or clauses.
and, but, because
Article
Specifies a noun (definite/indefinite).
a, an, the

05 — Interrogativo

Forming Questions

Invierte el orden: Verb + Subject + …?

Para respuestas cortas usamos: Yes, I am. / No, I'm not.

StatementQuestionShort YesShort No
I am a teacher.Am I a teacher?Yes, you are.No, you're not.
You are a student.Are you a student?Yes, I am.No, I'm not.
She is tired.Is she tired?Yes, she is.No, she isn't.
He is a doctor.Is he a doctor?Yes, he is.No, he isn't.
They are ready.Are they ready?Yes, they are.No, they aren't.
We are from Mexico.Are we from Mexico?Yes, we are.No, we're not.
06 — Aplicaciones

Usos del Verbo "To Be"

Identidad

My name is Ana. I am a designer.

Profesión

She is a lawyer. They are engineers.

Edad

I am 25 years old. He is 40.

Nacionalidad

We are Mexican. She is French.

Ubicación

The office is in Guadalajara. We are here.

Estado emocional

I am happy. He is nervous today.

Descripción física

She is tall. The building is old.

Clima / Tiempo

It is cold. It is sunny outside.

Tiempo / Hora

It is 8 a.m. Today is Monday.

08 — Práctica A

Ejercicio: Complete the Blanks

1
Fill in with am / is / are

I ____ a student.

El sujeto es "I" — solo tiene una forma posible.
✓ Respuesta: am → I am a student.
2
Fill in with am / is / are

She ____ a doctor.

He / She / It → misma forma.
✓ Respuesta: is → She is a doctor.
3
Fill in with am / is / are

We ____ from Mexico City.

You / We / They → misma forma.
✓ Respuesta: are → We are from Mexico City.
09 — Práctica B

Ejercicio: Multiple Choice

4
Choose the correct verb

It ____ very cold today.

"It" se usa para objetos, animales y clima.
✓ Respuesta: is → It is very cold today.
5
Choose the correct verb

They ____ not ready yet.

"They" es plural — igual que "We" y "You".
✓ Respuesta: are → They are not ready yet.
6
Choose the correct verb

My brother ____ a teacher.

"My brother" = he → tercera persona singular.
✓ Respuesta: is → My brother is a teacher.
10 — Práctica C

Ejercicio: Transform the Sentence

7
Make it negative (use contraction)

He is at home.

is + not → isn't. Mantén el resto igual.
✓ He isn't at home.
8
Turn into a question

You are a student.

Mueve el verbo al inicio.
✓ Are you a student?
9
Write the contraction form

They are not engineers.

are + not → aren't
✓ They aren't engineers.

12 — Saludos

Greetings & Introductions

El verbo to be es esencial para saludar, presentarse y conocer personas. Aquí los patrones más útiles:

Formal Greeting

Good morning. My name is Carlos Reyes. I am the project manager.

Casual Greeting

Hey! I'm Sofia. Nice to meet you! I'm from Monterrey.

Asking about others

What is your name? Where are you from? How old are you?

Typical Answers

My name is Ana. I am from Oaxaca. I am 22 years old.
